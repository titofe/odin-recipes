Tomato Basil Soup

Description

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 2 tablespoons heavy cream (optional)

Directions

  1. In a large saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the chopped tomatoes, broth, sugar, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and let simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and use an immersion blender or transfer to a blender to puree the soup until smooth.
  4. Stir in the fresh basil and heavy cream, if using.
  5. Reheat the soup until hot and serve immediately, garnished with additional fresh basil leaves, if desired.
