Tomato Soup Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 lbs. ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the chopped tomatoes, broth, sugar, basil, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Use an immersion blender or transfer the mixture to a blender to puree the soup until smooth.
  5. Return the soup to the saucepan and heat through. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.

Serve the hot soup with croutons or a drizzle of cream, if desired.
